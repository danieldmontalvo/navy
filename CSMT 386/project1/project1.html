Explain the importance of keywords and descriptions as meta tags


The importance of meta tags is that the search engines read them in order to compare if these keywords and the description are related to the visible content. Using Keywords in meta tags are important; regardless of all other words on your page, the user should be able to know what your site is all about when they read your keywords. Using the right keywords and descriptions will bring relevant searchers to your content on topics that'll serve their interest and solve their problems.
